Movie Ranking Project

The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is my favourite sci-fi movie of all-time. "Interstellar" is a visually stunning and thought-provoking sci-fi epic directed by Christopher Nolan. It has received praise for its ambitious storytelling, emotional depth, and breathtaking cinematography.

Next

Really cool sci-fi movie. "Next" (2007) is a sci-fi thriller starring Nicolas Cage, Julianne Moore, and Jessica Biel. The film follows Cris Johnson, a Las Vegas magician who has the ability to see a few minutes into his own future. When terrorists threaten to detonate a nuclear weapon, the FBI seeks his help to prevent the disaster.

Inception

Super beautiful film. Really artistic. "Inception," directed by Christopher Nolan, is widely regarded as a mind-bending masterpiece that blends action, sci-fi, and psychological thriller elements
